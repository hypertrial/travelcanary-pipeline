# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this community harassment-free for
everyone, regardless of background, identity, appearance, experience, or
ability. We will act in ways that create an open, welcoming, inclusive, and
healthy community.

## Our standards

Positive behavior includes empathy, respect for differing viewpoints,
constructive feedback, accepting responsibility, and focusing on the health of
the community. Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, sexualized language
or attention, trolling, insults, threats, publishing private information, or
other conduct inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and interactions that do
not align with these standards. Report conduct issues privately through the
maintainer contact described in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md). Reports will be
reviewed promptly, fairly, and confidentially.

This code applies in project spaces and when someone officially represents the
project. Consequences may include correction, warning, temporary exclusion, or
permanent exclusion, proportional to impact and repeated behavior.
